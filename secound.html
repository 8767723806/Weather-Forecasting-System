Disaster Management

Disaster management involves preparing for, responding to, and recovering from natural and man-made disasters. It includes risk assessment, emergency response planning, and implementing strategies to minimize damage and ensure public safety. Effective disaster management helps communities build resilience and recover efficiently from crises.

Emergency Response & Coordination

Ensuring a quick and effective response by coordinating efforts among agencies and first responders.

Resource & Relief Management

Efficient distribution of essential supplies and aid to affected areas.
